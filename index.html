The Local Business Directory Built for South African Communities

The Olideen Directory is a free local business directory built specifically for small-town and township businesses across KwaZulu-Natal — including Estcourt, Ladysmith, Newcastle, Pietermaritzburg, Colenso, Weenen, Dundee and surrounding areas. Whether you're looking for a home baker, a mechanic, a hair stylist, a tutor or a local transport provider, you'll find them here.

We built this for the side-hustler, the home-based business owner, the township entrepreneur — businesses that may not have a website or know how to get found on Google. Every listing is reviewed before going live, so you can trust what you find here. And listing is, and always will be, completely free. No hidden fees, no expiry, no sign-up required.
